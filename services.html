Services

What I do for organisations.

Six things, ordered by where I tend to be most useful. Civil-society groups usually pay nothing. Foundations and institutions usually pay a fee. The conversation is the same in both cases — you tell me what you're trying to change, I tell you what I think the route is.

🤝

Community organising

Power gets built, not declared. I help grassroots groups and NGOs turn a concern into a base, a base into a campaign, and a campaign into a decision someone has to make. Method, not motivation.

Pro-bono available
🎤

Workshop facilitation

Sessions on community organising, advocacy strategy, and how to read a piece of legislation without giving up. For NGOs, universities and groups that want to leave the room with something they can use on Monday.

Fee / pro-bono
📣

Campaign strategy & design

From a problem to a movement. I help organisations design campaigns that build coalitions, move institutions, and don't fall apart at the first push-back from the people they are trying to convince.

Fee / pro-bono
🌐

Coalition building

Most policy fights are won by the third or fourth ally, not the first. I help groups find common ground with the people they didn't think they could work with, and make those alliances structured enough to outlast a leadership change.

Fee / pro-bono
🔬

Policy analysis & research

Position papers, briefings and memos that actually get read. EU and international files made legible for boards, members and the journalists you are trying to brief. Footnotes included.

Fee-based
📱

Digital education & content

Short-form video and social strategy for organisations that want to reach beyond the room. Built around the format, the audience and the actual policy — not around the algorithm of the week.

Fee-based
