About Me

Campaign builder.
Sense-maker. Change architect.

I'm a campaign and advocacy professional with 10+ years of experience across civil society, EU-level advocacy, and political movements. Currently a Policy Officer at The Good Lobby Italia, where I promote human rights and work on civic space advocacy.

My background spans international economic history, chemistry (BSc), and multiple degrees in history & civilization. I've shaped European Parliament resolutions on Sudan and Tunisia, led large-scale campaigns with over 1,100 participants, and built coalitions across civil society.

I founded The Continental Brief — a Substack newsletter on EU politics and political economy — and create short-form educational video content on institutional dynamics and democratic accountability. I believe complex policy issues should be accessible to everyone.

Mission & Vision

Why this work matters.

Complex policy issues should not belong only to experts. Democracy is only as strong as the people who understand and participate in it.

"My work focuses on sense-making around European politics and economy, improving democracy, and promoting human and social rights. I believe complex policy issues should be accessible to everyone."
🧭

Sense-Making

Breaking down the complexity of EU institutions, political economy, and international dynamics into language that anyone can engage with. From newsletters to short-form video, I translate the machinery of power into accessible insight.

🗳️

Improving Democracy

Working to close the gap between citizens and the institutions that govern them. Through advocacy, coalition building, and civic education, I push for accountable, transparent, and participatory democratic systems in Europe and beyond.

⚖️

Human & Social Rights

From Sudan to civic space in Europe, I work to ensure human rights remain at the centre of policy decisions. Amplifying voices, building coalitions, and holding institutions to account — because rights require active defenders.
